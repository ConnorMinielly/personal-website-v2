Animation Fundamentals For Programmers Like Me And You

I did this talk a couple years back as an opportunity to chat about something I have a lot of passion for: animation! While not every aspect of traditional animation translates to the web, I think understanding the concepts that animators have used for the past 70 years to create charming and appealing motion art is really critical. So, that said, enjoy the talk! And sorry for the recording quality, we were trying a new venue that night and the AV setup was not as good as we were used to.
